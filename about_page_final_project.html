    About Our Project


    Our project focuses on bringing political transparency to the American public by creatively visualizing and analyzing Congressional campaign finance data from the Federal Election Commission. Free, fair, and open data is released in bulk by the FEC each year, yet the majority of Americans are not aware that this data is public and readily downloadable. This website is dedicated to useful, readily-accessible and user-friendly visualizations of the FEC's House & Senate campaign donation data.
